What the fuck is this?

It was meant to be a rust lib for Bwbl and now it's also a JS lib.

Just why ??

Because you know, today's protocols are really boring, but now with this insane lib and site you can just encode bytes and decode them back and I think that every optimized protocol should have that.

How it works ?

Type things in the left area and then press the magic encode button and boom your message can be understood by every transfem in the right side

If you're not a transfem and you can't understand an encoded message you can do the same process with the encoded message the left then press the decode button and bim badaboom decoded message to the right
